// What is ALFA MNT

Real-time geopolitical intelligence.
Verifiable track record.

ALFA MNT monitors world events as they happen, classifies them by severity and category through a military intelligence framework, and studies how they correlate with market movements in real time.


What separates the platform from a news feed is accountability. Every analysis is tracked against actual market outcomes and published in an open research record. No edits. No hindsight. No exceptions.

01
Real-Time Event Monitoring
Global events are detected, classified, and analyzed as they happen. Breaking developments are surfaced through severity-weighted alerts before they reach mainstream coverage.
02
Severity-Classified Analysis
Each event is analyzed for geopolitical significance, assigned a severity score, and mapped to historically correlated market sectors. Outputs include causal reasoning and counter-perspectives.
03
Verifiable Research Record
Every analysis tracked against real market outcomes. Confirmed or not confirmed, published openly. No cherry-picking, no hidden failures, no retroactive edits.
04
Built by a Veteran
Designed by a combat veteran and defense contractor who spent years reading chaotic environments in Iraq. The same analytical framework, applied to studying global markets.
